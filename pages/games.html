I n s i d i o u s

Insidious will be an immersive horror game, set
in Europe. You will be facing mysterious,
dangerous as well as truly haunting beings that go deep into the uncanny valley.

True Horror Insidious Games puts a lot of effort into researching psychology of humans and natural fear responses to simulate something truly uncanny.
High Quality High quality assets are made to ensure a great experience when playing the game.
Complex enemies The haunting creatures you will fight, act differently depending on the situation. A complex aggression system ensures a responsive experience.
Variety Insidious allows for a large variety of beings with many personalities. Therefore, you will never encounter the same creature twice.
